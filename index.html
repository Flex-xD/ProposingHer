In Your Silence, You Bloomed 🌺

You were quiet, and I was the loudest person in the room, I was literally your विलोम in every possible way, but that's one thing that I started thinking about jab jab I was chup. While the whole class around us demanded constant noise, endless chatter, from questions to answers to gossips to more gossips to sometimes silence, you didn't speak much but whenever you did, your words — the way a writer chooses each one — carefully, deliberately, meaningfully. I think these three words did described you back then. There's an elegance in your silence that most people will never understand, a depth that can only exist when someone truly thinks before they speak.

I've watched you turn into the loudest, most vibrant version of yourself with your small circle of friends, and it's the most beautiful contradiction I've ever witnessed (and I will ever witness). You were not quiet because you had nothing to say but you were quiet because you had so much to say and you were just waiting for the right moment, the right people, the right words. I was never a listener, nor an observer, but you made me one without even wanting to.
